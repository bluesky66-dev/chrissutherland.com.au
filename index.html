Why.

Almost every waking day, we feel energised by the thought of solving business problems and creating opportunities for our clients. Creating fascinating brands and original experiences is our cup of tea.

This agency was born out of the experience of Chris’s time working at some of the largest global advertising agencies and marketing departments in Australia. Over the years he has learned that success doesn’t just happen. It takes a lifetime journey of constant improvement that keeps you humble, and that crafting great ideas and meaningful messaging comes from great teamwork.

That’s why Chris has had success on many communication projects, ranging from food, government, and security — through to — the automotive and retail sectors.

“I believe that advertising is about being unique and communicating messages that resonate with people and at Chris Sutherland, we believe the world would be a better place if there was less clutter and more meaning.”
- Chris Sutherland
